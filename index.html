For decades, Microsoft web browsers have been driving developers to the brink of madness and in many cases, to their early graves. Lucky for us, in the midwestern town of Cincinnati, Gaslight employs only the best and brightest developers, those that stand vigilant and steadfast to combat this menace on the graveyard of past (non-Gaslight) developers.

Enter Our Hero, Super Chris!

With aid from the Microsoft web browser kryptonite; the almighty Gaslight logo, can Super Chris defeat the plague that has claimed so many before him and save us all from certain web-browsing doom? Click the button below to find out!

Directions to play:


  • Use the spacebar to jump.
